Frequently asked questions

Will the act provide all the necessary equipment?

Most brass / oompah / marching bands are self-contained and provide their own equipment which is suitable for typical situations. Even though the act generally plays acoustically, if your event is to be held in a large venue, or you have more than 300 guests, we recommend that additional sound equipment should be used. Always ask your musicians about their sound system requirements.

What’s the Best Brass Band for Hire in the UK?

While we’ve got over 40 brilliant brass bands for hire to choose from, we wouldn’t possibly be able to select the best. The best brass band for your event depends on what the event is, the vibe you want and the type of music you love. We have classical brass bands for hire across the UK and Bavarian style Oompah bands too. So have a browse, a listen, check out prices and pick your favourite.

How long do brass / oompah / marching bands usually perform for?

Each event is different but the typical performance time for brass / oompah / marching bands is 2 x 45 minutes sets within a 2 hours period. Some acts are happy to play longer sets or additional sets. Often, breaks can be negotiated but you should be aware that the price may increase if the musicians need to be onsite for a longer period of time.

Do brass / oompah / marching bands perform outdoors?

You should always check whether or not your act is prepared to play outdoors. Weather conditions such as wind, rain, or extreme temperatures can affect the performance and the instruments. If you are planning an outdoor event you should be prepared to offer a flat surface for performers as well as a cover in case of unexpected rain.

How far in advance do we need to book your services?

Booking brass / oompah / marching bands as early as possible increases the chance of securing their availability on your event date, particularly during busy times of the year. However, many acts also accept last minute bookings, and we have successfully arranged performances for clients with just a few hours' notice.

How much do brass / oompah / marching bands cost?

The cost of hiring musicians can vary significantly depending on the type of act. The average price is currently £1224. Budget-level acts may be available for as low as £400, while top-tier acts can command prices as high as £2050.

When booking these bands:

It may sound obvious, but If you're holding a public event, don't automatically assume it will be okay for you to have a marching band parading around. Consider any noise restrictions and regulations that may be in place, and always obtain the correct permissions if you are hoping to have your band march on the road (even the quietest ones!).

Prices for marching bands can vary hugely- some of them will be happy to send along as few as 5 members. On the other hand, others may add up to closer to 15.

Brass / Oompah / Marching Bands repertoires

Some marching bands will recite more traditional music, like hymns or carols. Others will will adopt a different style, like oompah bands or samba groups. New Orleans jazz groups will try to create a true Mardi Gras style celebration! Recently, we've also seen a big trend in brass bands re-arranging funk and soul classics, like The Hot 8 Brass Band. Some bands will be able to take a request or two, but remember that it will take a long time to arrange and rehearse any material for a large (or small!) brass band, so make sure you speak to your contact in the group as far in advance as possible, and preferably provide the sheet music.
